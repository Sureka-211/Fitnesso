Boiled Egg Sandwich

Main Ingredients:

  1. Brown Bread Slice
  2. Raw Egg
  3. Tomato
  4. Onion
  5. Green Chillies
  6. Pepper

1 Boil the egg in salt water for 10 to 12 minutes.
2 Peel and cut it into small dice.
3 Fine chop the onion, tomato, green chilli, and coriander leaves.
4 Take a mixing bowl, add dices of boiled egg along with onion, tomato, coriander, green chilli, salt and pepper. Mix it well to combine everything and set aside.
5 Place the mixture on a bread slice and sandwich it with another slice. Repeat the same with other bread slices.
6 Heat a pan, add a little oil. Place the sandwich and sear on other sides until toasted and golden in color. Slice it into 4 pieces.
7 Serve warm with green chutney.
